### UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-border Train Journey (PagoPA)
PagoPA as Wallet Provider, lead for this UC.
Trenitalia as a train service provider acting as Relying Party for the verification of the passenger Identity (age, gender) in order to certificate their eligibility to have access to dedicated passengers' fare on their app (for instance, women reserved cabins).
Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato as the italian official issuer for PID and eventually DTC. 
Starting from the definition of the use case, we conducted bilateral meetings and we worked together on shared documents. The final aim was to find an alternative solution to the actual processes in which integrating the Wallet infrastructure, providing users with the full control of their identity.
