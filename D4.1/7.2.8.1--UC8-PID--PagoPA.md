#### PID (Personal Identification Data)
**PID** is used to verify identity matching between the ticket data and the user requesting it. It is issued by the Italian official Public Issuer, Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato and it is verified by Trenitalia, acting as RP.

_Data model for PID:_
- family_name
- given_name
- birth_date
- birth_place
- nationality
- gender
