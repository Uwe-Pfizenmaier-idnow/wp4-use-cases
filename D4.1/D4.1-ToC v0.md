### 4. Current Landscape and Baseline Analysis (All UC leads → GRNET)

> Findings of the T4.2 stock-taking activity "Analysis of existing processes, technical implementations supporting online services, identity matching and management." - 4.1 Current Processes and Existing Implementations (All UC leads → GRNET)

  > Describes how each sector currently handles the processes that WP4 aims to transform with the EUDIW. For each sector: existing digital infrastructure (APIs, apps, identity systems) that WP4 implementations will need to interoperate.

- 4.2 Identity Matching and Management (All UC leads → GRNET)

  > How organisations in each sector currently match a person to their booking, ticket, or enrolment? Current approaches and their limitations (transliteration errors, name discrepancies across documents, no interoperable identity layer across providers or borders). ### 7. Description of the Ecosystem: Actors and Roles (All UC leads → GRNET)

> Consolidated view of all actor types that participate in WP4 use cases.

- 7.1 Wallet Holders (Travellers, Hotel Guests, Students) (GRNET)

  > The different user profiles relevant to WP4 that carry the EUDIW: (business traveller, tourist, student, traveller with special needs) and what they need from the wallet across the three scenarios.

- 7.2 Wallet Providers (GRNET)

  > The Member State entities responsible for providing the official EUDIW application to citizens. Wallet providers from participating Member States.

- 7.3 PID Providers (GRNET)

  > The government authorities that issue the foundational PID credential into the wallet. Describes which national authorities act as PID providers in the pilot and what PID attributes are available.

- 7.4 Credential Issuers (All UC leads → GRNET)

  > The organisations that issue sector-specific EAAs into users' wallets: airlines, hotels, train/bus tickets, GYSC issuing student ID cards, universities issuing ERUA/ERASMUS credentials

- 7.5 Relying Parties (All UC leads → GRNET)

  > The organisations that verify credentials presented by wallet holders to grant access to services.

- 7.6 Trust Service Providers (GRNET)

  > Entities providing trust services (qualified certificates, timestamping, etc.) that WP4 actors rely on.

- 7.7 Mapping of Actors to Use Cases and Scenarios (All UC leads → GRNET)

  > A summary table mapping each APTITUDE actor to the UCs they participate in, indicating their role (wallet issuer, PID provider, credential issuer, relying party).
