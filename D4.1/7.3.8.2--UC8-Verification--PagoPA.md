#### Verification
UC8 core is the verification of the identity of the passenger, via PID or DTC stored in PagoPA Wallet, directly by Trenitalia app. In this case two types of verification are involved:
- **Remote verification on Tranitalia app during the booking process:** The passenger initiates a remote sharing process via their EUDI Wallet, providing a Verifiable Attestation of their PID/DTC. The platform automatically validates the attribute and verifies the identity, reserving a cabin for women if needed. 
- **Proximity presentation on board of the train**: the day of the journey, Trenitalia staff requires an identification for the passenger, who will present their QR-code to the verification tool.
